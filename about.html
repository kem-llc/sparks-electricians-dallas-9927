Our story

Your Trusted Dallas Electrician Since 2008

To provide safe, reliable, and affordable electrical services that exceed customer expectations, while maintaining the highest standards of professionalism and integrity.

Sparks Electricians was founded in 2008 by master electrician John Sparks, who saw a need for honest, high-quality electrical work in Dallas. Starting with just a truck and a dream, John built a reputation for reliability and transparency. Today, our team of licensed professionals serves hundreds of homes and businesses across the metroplex.

We believe in treating every customer like family. That means showing up on time, communicating clearly, and standing behind our work. Our 4.8-star rating and hundreds of 5-star reviews reflect our commitment to excellence. Whether it's a simple repair or a complex installation, we bring the same dedication to every job.
