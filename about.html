Our story

Dallas's Home for Quality Electrical Work

To provide safe, reliable, and affordable electrical services that exceed expectations, every time.

Sparks Electricians was founded in 2005 by master electrician Mark Sparks, who saw a need for honest, high-quality electrical work in the Dallas area. Starting with just a truck and a commitment to doing things right, Mark built a reputation for reliability and craftsmanship that spread by word of mouth.

Today, Sparks Electricians serves hundreds of homeowners and businesses across Dallas, from historic homes in Lakewood to modern offices in Uptown. Our team of licensed electricians shares Mark's original vision: treat every customer like family, never cut corners, and always leave the job better than you found it.
