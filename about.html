Our story

Your Trusted Dallas Electricians

To provide safe, reliable, and affordable electrical services to every home and business in Dallas.

Sparks Electricians was founded in 2009 by master electrician Tom Sparks, who saw a need for honest, high-quality electrical work in the Dallas area. Starting with a single truck, Tom built a reputation for integrity and craftsmanship, one job at a time. Over the years, we've grown into a team of over 20 licensed electricians, but our core values remain the same: treat every customer like family, and never cut corners.

Today, we serve residential and commercial clients across Dallas, from Lakewood to Uptown. We invest in ongoing training and the latest tools to ensure our work meets the highest standards. Whether it's a simple outlet repair or a complex commercial installation, we approach every project with the same dedication to excellence.
