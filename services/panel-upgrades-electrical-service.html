Panel Upgrades & Electrical Service

Upgrade your electrical panel for increased safety, capacity, and efficiency.

Overview

Older panels can be a fire hazard and limit your electrical usage. Our panel upgrade service brings your home up to modern standards, supporting new appliances, electronics, and HVAC systems. We handle everything from 100-amp to 400-amp upgrades.

What's included

  • Increased safety with modern breakers and wiring
  • Supports additional circuits for renovations or additions
  • Energy-efficient designs that may lower utility bills
