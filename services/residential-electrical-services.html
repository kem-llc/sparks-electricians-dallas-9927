Common questions

How do I know if my panel needs upgrading?

Signs include frequent breaker trips, flickering lights, or an older panel (e.g., Federal Pacific). We offer free inspections to assess your panel's condition.

Do you provide emergency services?

Yes, we offer 24/7 emergency electrical services for issues like power outages, sparking outlets, or exposed wires.
